Help & support

How to use Pulse

Tell Pulse how many days until your exam and take the short knowledge check. Then Pulse builds a review path sized to your time and readiness. In each concept a quick question checks you — a wrong answer opens a short reteach (a video and a reading) before you try again.

Common questions

The syllabus PDF won't load. Some browsers block inline PDFs — use the “open this PDF in a new tab” link, or view the online (https) version.

Can I switch paths or start over? Click the Pulse logo (top-left) to return to the start and rebuild your plan.

Is my progress saved? Yes! Saved when you not even noticed. Clearing your browser storage resets it.

Technical issues

Pulse will be very appreaciate if you find something that doesn't make any sense. Email peizhel@unc.edu and describe what happened. Every single error helps massive improvements for Pulse!
